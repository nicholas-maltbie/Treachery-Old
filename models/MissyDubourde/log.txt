Inspiriation for model from Missy minifigure from Betrayal at house on the hill game.

Base model guide from

https://en.wikibooks.org/wiki/Blender_3D:_Noob_to_Pro/Modeling_a_Human_Character_-_Modeling

Used this video to model face and get help to make mesh/sculpt

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OOc4ATqZetk

In order to get a defined face with low polys, I used this tutorial

https://cgi.tutsplus.com/articles/sculpt-model-and-texture-a-low-poly-skull-in-blender--cg-7

Used this video to find a method to unwrap an entire human body.

https://vimeo.com/9866038

In order to model the hair, I used this tutorial

https://cgi.tutsplus.com/tutorials/female-character-modeling-in-blender-part-5--cms-21211
